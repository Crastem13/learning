MaxiIRT IR100

The MaxiIRT IR100 Thermal Imaging Camera measures from -20° C to 450° C (-4° F to 842° F), making heat fluctuation, loss, or buildup visible to the human eye. The dual camera overlay feature allows you to view thermal images over standard images, creating easily identifiable images. The IR100 combines high resolution, auto-focus, a 0.98 second refresh rate, and Wi-Fi image transfer to your PC or mobile device, including your MaxiSYS tablet, for a swift and accurate diagnosis. Adjustable emissivity settings and a choice of five color pallets helps customize the user experience. Troubleshoot brakes, exhaust, heated seats, HVAC and more in seconds. The IR100 includes a 1-Year Warranty.
